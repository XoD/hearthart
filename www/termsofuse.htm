DESIGNS

Designs using natural materials or glass are not recommended for working fireplaces. These designs can handle mild heat exposure such as temperatures produced from gas log fire places; however, to ensure the safety of your screen while the fire place is in use, please pull it away from the source of heat by at least twenty inches. All fire screens can be customized in regard to size and metal finishes. Some are available in a selection of other natural materials. Please inquire about options in regard to natural materials for individual designs.

The natural materials used in our designs are fragile and subject to breakage; please handle with care.

PATINA FINISHES

Sample patina finishes supplied will show general direction of patinas. The patina process is a chemical oxidation subject to various factors such as weather, exact metal composition, humidity and temperature. Exact color matching is not guaranteed.
